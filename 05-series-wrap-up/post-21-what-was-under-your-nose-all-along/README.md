# Post 21 Companion Toolkit

This folder supports the final wrap-up post in the Hyper-V Renaissance series.

The purpose of this companion is not to introduce new deployment scripts. It is to help readers turn the series into a platform decision and an execution path.

## Contents

- `guides/series-to-execution-roadmap.md`
  Maps the major posts in the series to the decisions and implementation work they support.
- `checklists/final-platform-decision-checklist.md`
  A concise final review checklist for teams deciding between staying on the current VMware path, moving to Azure Local, or standardizing on Hyper-V.
- `worksheets/final-platform-comparison.csv`
  A lightweight worksheet for summarizing fit, cost posture, hardware posture, and operational impact.

## How To Use This Folder

1. Read the final wrap-up post first.
2. Use the decision checklist with infrastructure, finance, and operations stakeholders.
3. Fill in the comparison worksheet with your own environment data.
4. Use the roadmap guide to jump back into the specific posts and toolkit folders that support your chosen path.

## Series Position

This is the conclusion to the 21-post Hyper-V Renaissance series. It assumes you have already reviewed the capability, cost, architecture, operations, hybrid, and automation posts.
